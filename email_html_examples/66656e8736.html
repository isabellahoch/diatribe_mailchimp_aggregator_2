September 27 - 28, 2017 
d17: The Second Executive Innovation Lab
on Diabetes and Pre-Diabetes
Sponsored by The diaTribe Foundation 
Last year, The diaTribe Foundation hosted “d16: Executive Innovation Lab on Diabetes and Obesity” a highly selective, two-day gathering of brilliant medical, scientific, policy, and education leaders from healthcare and technology. Participants worked on changing the conversation and recommending actions regarding type 2 diabetes and pre-diabetes, as we are not satisfied with the outcomes or the direction of the biggest public health problems of our time.
In 2017, we will continue the conversation to drive greater innovation, collaboration, and impact in diabetes and obesity.
And we’d like you to join us. We will: 
  • ARTICULATE a narrative of the diabetes landscape, discuss key obstacles preventing progress, and identify the most pressing and addressable problems in the field;
     
  • CONDUCT a broad analysis of the diabetes ecosystem, including: systems, behavior change/stigma, public attitudes/social norms, and prevention;
     
  • PINPOINT bright spots and gaps in diabetes and ask: what is currently successful, what needs improvement, and what is most scalable?
     
  • BUILD on the highly innovative, systems-levels solutions conceptualized by d16 participants while also expanding on the initiatives showcased in the Consensus of Ideas, all of which  can reduce the societal burden of diabetes and heighten the urgency  for action around the epidemic; and
     
  • EXPLORE how design thinking and cross-sector collaboration can help us develop integrated, systems-level approaches to diabetes prevention and behavior change.

In addition to Adam’s article, we’re also featuring two other articles in this week’s issue – a Trial Watch on Jardiance, an SGLT-2 inhibitor, and the third part in our diabetes support series, “Get Confident About Diabetes Management in Six Weeks.”
 
Can Jardiance, an SGLT-2 inhbitor, protect against heart failure? A new study is investigating whether the drug can decrease risk of heart failure in people with type 2 diabetes. Jardiance was the first diabetes drug approved to reduce the risk of heart-related death, including from events like heart attacks and strokes; this study could now help determine whether the drug is also protective for heart failure, a chronic condition when the heart cannot circulate enough blood to the body.

2. BREAKOUT GROUP QUESTIONS 
We’re excited to share the breakout group questions we’ll be discussing during the afternoon portion of our innovation lab (see below)! If you have preferences for which breakout question you would like to discuss - please email us (you can just send the letter or letters). We'll have two rounds of discussion and ideation - each question has very interesting follow up questions. 
  • A - How might we mobilize patients and leaders to reduce stigma and improve public attitudes and social norms surrounding diabetes and prediabetes?
  • B - How might we re-design the acute-care- oriented healthcare system to better serve chronic disease management?
  • C - How might we make prevention of diabetes and prediabetes a priority in the health system and beyond?
  • D - How might we design programs that result in behavior change?
  • E - How might we change/create policies that promote health and wellness?

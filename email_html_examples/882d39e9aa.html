Greetings from diaTribe!

On July 21, the FDA will hold an advisory committee meeting to discuss whether CGM should be officially approved for making diabetes treatment decisions. At The diaTribe Foundation, we are working hard to support this label update and ensure that the meeting is a huge success.

Lend your support here; it only takes 30 seconds!  Learn more about this important issue in a one-minute video from our own Adam Brown.  Thank you so much in advance for your help to us in sharing this. 
 
This upcoming meeting has very important implications for people with diabetes in terms of potential Medicare coverage, patient safety and education (for those already dosing insulin off CGM), future products that reduce the need for fingerstick calibration, patients’ and providers’ willingness to use CGM, and potentially even reimbursement.
 
To support this label update, The diaTribe Foundation has written a letter to the FDA conveying the importance of using CGM for making treatment decisions. It details the scientific rationale behind the insulin dosing request, and has been reviewed by over a dozen scientists, advocates, and other key opinion leaders in the field. Our goal is to collect over 7,500 signatures on this letter before submitting it to the FDA by the July 13 deadline – we need your help desperately to achieve our final goal! We are now over 5,000 signatures but there's a lot of work to do to convey the seriousness of this to FDA!

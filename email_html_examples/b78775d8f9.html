The Consensus of Ideas: As noted, the d16 Executive Innovation Lab produced the Consensus of Ideas, which features twelve ideas that could address some of the most pressing challenges in diabetes. At d17, we look forward to diving into these ideas and to refine the thinking, all to the end that we can define together what is most actionable, investable, and achievable.

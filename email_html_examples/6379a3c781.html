If you'd like to join us Friday, please let us know; I believe that Veronica has reached out to those who are local to give you the calendar for these for the spring and we understand everyone is busy but just wanted to make sure you had this in case  you are interested in meeting her. 

As always, if you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact Ben (ben.pallant@diaTribe.org), or Mallory (mallory.erickson@diaTribe.org) or me in advance of the meeting. We can't wait to see you very soon!

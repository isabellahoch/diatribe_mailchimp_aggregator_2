Thank You from The diaTribe Foundation and Taking Control of Your Diabetes!
 


​
Dear Forum attendee,

Thank you for making our 12th Annual Diabetes Forum and Reception a success! We are so pleased that you came to listen to and engage with the insights of our distinguished panel of opinion leaders. The diaTribe and TCOYD teams look forward to seeing how the connections forged at the Forum will impact the healthcare landscape and improve the lives of those living with diabetes and prediabetes for years to come! 

With this in mind, we would greatly appreciate your response to a short, less than 2-minute survey about your experience. Your feedback is essential to the success of our events, and we would love to learn how next year’s Forum can be even better. As an added incentive, we will select one person who takes the survey at random to receive a free ticket to next year’s event!

Thank you again for your support, and we look forward to seeing you in San Francisco for ADA 2019. Please see below for a few choice shots of the event!

Very best,

The diaTribe Foundation and Taking Control of Your Diabetes Teams

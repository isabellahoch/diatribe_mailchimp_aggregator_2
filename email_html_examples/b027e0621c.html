With Adam Brown's book Bright Spots and Landmines giving me way more motivation about eating well, I'm thrilled to have these recipes by Catherine to turn to! 
 
Can you guess the most underrated – but most effective – diabetes exercise strategy? It’s simple – walking! In this most recent Adam’s Corner, Adam makes the case for walking as one of the most effective ways to correct high blood sugars. Adam shares even more exercise-related tips and tricks in his new book, Bright Spots and Landmines: The Diabetes Guide I Wish Someone Had Handed Me. Please let us know if you don’t already have a copy, and we’ll send you one right away! I’ve been SO moved by Adam and walking that I’m even using the “Human” app – I’m at 72 minutes walking today (literally, this app counts ALL your walking!), which puts me at #39 of 142 folks on the app in SF right this moment. Let me know!

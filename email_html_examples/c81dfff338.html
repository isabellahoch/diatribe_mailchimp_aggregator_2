3. DR. BJ FOGG’s BEHAVIOR DESIGN SYNTHESIS   

We were honored to introduce you to the behavioral thinking of Dr. BJ Fogg. Our lives will never be the same now that we understand the thinking behind fish, frogs, butterflies, and worms. With our new knowledge of habits and behaviors, let’s work on ways to help support all of the Claras in our daily lives!
